Quick Ratio Guide

Question: Can the company pay its bills?

Equation: (Cash & Equivalents + Receivables) ÷ Current Liabilities

Guide:

  • Fragile = <1.0
  • Robust = 1 to 1.5
  • Antifragile = 1.5+

Current Ratio Guide

Question: How well does the company manage its assets?

Equation: Current Assets ÷ Current Liabilities

Guide:

  • Fragile = <0.7
  • Robust = 0.7 - 2
  • Antifragile = 2+

Debt-to-Equity Ratio Guide

Question: How much leverage is the company using?

Equation: Total Liabilities ÷ Shareholder Equity

Guide:

  • Fragile = ≥2.0
  • Robust = 0.7 - 2
  • Antifragile = <0.7

Goodwill to Total Assets Ratio Guide

Question: Is the company growing organically?

Equation: Goodwill ÷ Total Assets

Guide:

  • Fragile = >50%
  • Robust = 10%-50%
  • Antifragile = <10%
